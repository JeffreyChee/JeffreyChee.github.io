Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 7 Jan, 2014

This privacy policy sets out how Happy Labs Pte Ltd (“Happy Labs”) uses and protects any information that we collect when you use our products, services, and websites (collectively, “Services”). By using our Services, you agree to our Privacy Policy.

Happy Labs is committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. Should we ask you to provide personally-identifiable information when using our Services, be assured that it will only be used in accordance with this Privacy Policy. Otherwise, all information collected are non-identifying.

Happy Labs may periodically update this Privacy Policy. You should check this page from time to time to ensure that you are happy with any changes.

What we collect

Unless otherwise specified, all data gathered is non-identifiable, non-personal and aggregated.

  • Data collected using Third-party Platforms:
      1. Tracking: We use Google Analytics to help analyze and optimize our Services. Any usage data gathered is subject to the Google Privacy Policy.
      2. Social Media: We use Facebook and Twitter to allow you to share news with your friends. Any usage data gathered is subject to Facebook Platform Policies and Twitter's Developer Rules of The Road respectively.
      3. Ad-Serving Technologies: As part of the Service, we use a variety of third party ad-serving technologies. Such platforms may use cookies, web beacons, and other technologies to collect data, including but not limited to usage, demographic, geographic location, device model, device ID, where applicable. In addition, these third-party ad serving platforms may aggregate the data with other data on their platforms, and such data is governed by their own privacy policies. Happy Labs is unable to control third party technologies.
  • Data collected by Happy Labs:
      1. We will have access to your email addresses if you send us a customer support email in order to help answer your queries.
      2. From time to time, Happy Labs may run contest giveaways. For details on data collected during contest giveaways, please refer to the relevant contest Terms and Conditions.
What we do with the information we gather

We require this information to understand your needs and provide you with a better service, in particular for the following reasons:

  • 1. We may use the information to improve our Services.
  • 2. We may use the information to customize our Services according to your interests./li>
  • 3. The advertising companies mentioned above may customize their ads according to your non-personal, non-identifying, aggregated information.
  • 4. We may contact you by email.
  • 5. We will never sell your information.
Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. In order to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure, we have put in place suitable physical and electronic procedures. Happy Labs also adopts internal personal data protection policies and practices to safeguard and secure the information we collect.
